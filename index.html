What it means

While playing the lottery isn't dangerous, you aren't very likely to win big. In fact, you have more of a chance of dying on the way to purchase your ticket than winning the Powerball jackpot. It doesn't mean that you shouldn't play, that's your decision; however, you should have realistic expectations regarding winning.

How it works

The data used for the calculations was sourced from the following sources:

  • Powerball Odds
  • Fatality Rates by State
The probability of dying per mile in a state is calculated by dividing the total number of vehicular deaths by the total number of vehicle miles driven.

Disclaimer

This website is intended to illustrate just how low the probability of winning the Powerball is not to accurately estimate the probability of dying while driving. The fatality probability should be taken with a grain of salt and is only illustrative in nature, and by no means an accurate estimate of the probability that a person would die in a given mile. That figure is an oversimplified estimate of the true probability of dying in a mile of driving.
